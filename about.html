WHO I AM

About

I am currently a student at Methacton High School in Eagleville, PA and will be looking forward to graduating in 2016. In school, I participate in various activities such as Math Club, the Tennis team, and the school newspaper (The Windy Hill). As a part of the newspaper, I created an organization called Humans of Methacton where I post pictures of the Methacton community. These pictures are featured in the school newspaper, facebook, and the Windy Hill website. Outside of school, I love to learn new things. Some things I have taken interest in include cooking, web development, gaming, and even trying to do all 3 things at once.
